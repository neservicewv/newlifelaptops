IMAGES FOLDER
=============

Place your logo file here:
  images/logo.png         <-- Your New Life Laptops logo (PNG with transparency works best)

For product images:
  images/products/        <-- Put product photos here

Naming convention for product images:
  dell-latitude-e7470.jpg
  hp-elitebook-840-g4.jpg
  etc.

Then update the 'image' field in js/products.js for each product.
Example:
  image: 'images/products/dell-latitude-e7470.jpg',

Recommended image size: 800x600px or 4:3 ratio
Formats: JPG or PNG
