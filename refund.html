Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. Here’s how returns and refunds work.

Last Updated: June 2026  |  Effective: June 2026

At New Life Laptops, we stand behind every device we sell. We strive to accurately describe all products so you know exactly what you’re getting before you buy. If something isn’t right, we want to make it right.

1. Return Window

You may request a return within 30 days of the delivery date. After 30 days, all sales are considered final unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

2. Eligible Returns

Returns will be accepted if:

  • The device has a hardware defect or malfunction that was not disclosed in the product listing.
  • The device does not match the specifications listed at time of sale.
  • The device arrived damaged due to shipping (must be reported within 5 business days of delivery with photos).

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following are not eligible for return:

  • Devices with cosmetic issues that were clearly disclosed in the product condition grade and description.
  • Devices that have been physically damaged, dropped, or exposed to liquid after delivery.
  • Devices that have had unauthorized repairs or modifications performed after purchase.
  • Software issues caused by the buyer’s use, downloads, or account configuration.
  • Items returned without prior written authorization from New Life Laptops.

4. How to Start a Return

To begin a return, contact us within your return window:

  • Email: info@newlifelaptops.com
  • Subject line: Return Request – [Your Order / Device Name]
  • Include your name, device name, reason for return, and photos if applicable.

We will respond within 2 business days with return instructions. Do not ship a device back without first receiving return authorization — unauthorized returns may not be processed.

5. Return Shipping

  • If the return is due to our error (wrong item or undisclosed defect), we will cover return shipping costs.
  • For all other returns, the buyer is responsible for return shipping costs.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method. New Life Laptops is not responsible for items lost in transit.

6. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned device, we will notify you within 3 business days. If the return is approved:

  • A full or partial refund will be issued depending on the circumstances and condition of the returned item.
  • Refunds are processed via the original payment method or an agreed alternative.
  • Refunds may take 3–7 business days to appear, depending on your payment provider.

7. Exchanges

If you would prefer an exchange over a refund, we are happy to work with you to find a suitable replacement from our current inventory, subject to availability.

8. Contact Us

Questions about our refund policy? Reach us at info@newlifelaptops.com or visit our Contact page. We are committed to resolving any issues fairly and promptly.
