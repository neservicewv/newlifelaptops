Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. Here’s how returns and refunds work.

Last Updated: June 2026  |  Effective: June 2026

At New Life Laptops, we stand behind every device we sell.

1. Return Window

You may request a return within 30 days of the delivery date. After 30 days, all sales are considered final unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

2. Eligible Returns

  • The device has a hardware defect not disclosed in the product listing.
  • The device does not match the specifications listed at time of sale.
  • The device arrived damaged due to shipping (must be reported within 5 business days with photos).

3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Devices with cosmetic issues clearly disclosed in the product listing.
  • Devices physically damaged after delivery.
  • Devices with unauthorized repairs or modifications after purchase.
  • Software issues caused by the buyer’s use.

4. How to Start a Return

Contact us within your return window at neservicewv@gmail.com with your name, device name, reason for return, and photos if applicable. We will respond within 2 business days.

5. Return Shipping

  • If the return is due to our error, we will cover return shipping costs.
  • For all other returns, the buyer is responsible for return shipping.

6. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned device, we will notify you within 3 business days. Refunds may take 3–7 business days to appear depending on your payment provider.

7. Contact Us

Questions? Contact us or email neservicewv@gmail.com.
