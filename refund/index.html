Refund & Warranty Policy

Here’s what’s covered and how we handle issues after your purchase.

Last Updated: June 2026  |  Effective: June 2026

At New Life Laptops, we stand behind our work. Every device we sell goes through our 5-step restoration process, and we want you to feel confident in your purchase.

1. Limited Warranty

All devices sold by New Life Laptops come with a 14-day limited warranty from the date of delivery. This warranty covers defects or failures that are a direct result of our restoration process, including:

  • Hardware failures caused by faulty components installed or handled by us during restoration.
  • Issues arising from our repair or refurbishment work that were not present or disclosed at the time of sale.
  • Software configuration errors introduced during our setup process.

This is a limited warranty — it only covers problems that can be reasonably attributed to work performed by New Life Laptops. It does not cover issues caused by the buyer or normal wear after delivery.

2. What Is Not Covered

  • Physical damage that occurs after the device is delivered to you.
  • Liquid or water damage of any kind.Issues resulting from unauthorized repairs, modifications, or third-party software.
  • Normal wear and tear, cosmetic changes, or battery degradation that occurs during use.
  • Buyer-caused software issues, including virus infections, accidental deletion, or incorrect settings.
  • Cosmetic imperfections that were clearly disclosed in the product listing at time of sale.

3. How to Make a Warranty Claim

If you believe your device has an issue covered under this warranty, contact us within 14 days of delivery at neservicewv@gmail.com with the following:

  • Your name and the device you purchased.
  • A description of the issue.
  • Photos or video showing the problem, if applicable.

We will respond within 2 business days to assess your claim and determine the appropriate resolution, which may include repair, replacement, or refund at our discretion.

4. Return Shipping

  • If the issue is determined to be covered under this warranty, we will cover the cost of return shipping.
  • If the issue is found to be outside the scope of this warranty, return shipping costs are the buyer’s responsibility.

5. Refunds

If a warranty claim is approved and the issue cannot be resolved through repair or replacement, a refund may be issued. Once we receive and inspect the returned device, we will notify you within 3 business days. Refunds may take 3–7 business days to process depending on your payment provider.

6. All Sales After 14 Days

After the 14-day warranty period has passed, all sales are considered final. We are happy to answer questions or offer guidance beyond that window, but we are unable to offer refunds or warranty repairs for issues that arise after the coverage period ends.

Questions? Contact us or email neservicewv@gmail.com.
